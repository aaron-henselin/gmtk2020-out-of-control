=header=
Anti-HelloWorld

=description=
Outputing "HELLO, WORLD" is the introductory exercise for every programming language out there.
Seems fitting that the tutorial is then to subvert a HELLO, WORLD program.

=wincondition=
description:Success is achieved when the output has at least one line of text that is not the phrase 'HELLO WORLD.'
test:!HELLO   WORLD
